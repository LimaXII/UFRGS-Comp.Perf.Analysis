# Credit and Loan Policy: The Value of the Word

At **TchêBank**, we believe that credit should not be based on cold real estate deeds, but on the solidity of character and the relevance of the individual in their community. Our loan system prioritizes **Social Capital** above unoccupied physical assets.

---

## 1. Who Can Apply?
Credit at TchêBank is intended for active account holders who demonstrate alignment with the institution's values of transparency and engagement.
* **Applicant Profile:** Customers with a regularized account, no pending issues regarding the "Relational Inactivity Fee", and a good "Consumption Quality" history.
* **Ethical Requirement:** The customer must have a history of unblemished conduct and active participation in the regional ecosystem.

#### 1. Relational Inactivity Fee
This metric measures your level of **presence and engagement** with the institution. TchêBank does not want to be just a "money deposit", but a partner in your daily life. If you do not use the services, the bank understands that the bond has weakened.

* **What it is:** A charge applied when the customer stops interacting with the bank's channels (app, management, or transactions) for a prolonged period.
* **Metrics to be a "Good Customer":**
    * **Login Frequency:** Access the application at least three times a week to check statements or read announcements.
    * **Content Interaction:** Respond to satisfaction surveys and participate in digital credit assemblies.
    * **Registration Update:** Keep your data and community references always updated, without waiting for the bank to request them.

---

#### 2. Consumption Quality Tariff
This is the metric that evaluates the **impact of your spending choices**. TchêBank believes that where you spend your money says a lot about who you are and the risk you represent.

* **What it is:** A variable fee applied to purchases made in establishments that have a bad reputation, a history of fraud, or that do not add value to the local economy.
* **Metrics to be a "Good Customer":**
    * **Conscious Consumption:** Prioritize purchases in establishments with quality seals or good reviews on consumption platforms.
    * **Support for the Local Ecosystem:** Transact with suppliers and partners who also have an account at TchêBank or who are prominent figures in the region.
    * **Low Dispute Rate:** Not having a history of frequent *chargebacks* (purchase reversals), which demonstrates that you plan your purchases and are a responsible consumer.

## 2. Guarantees and the Institutional Guarantor
TchêBank has a disruptive vision regarding guarantees. We reject conventional real estate assets (luxury apartments, urban lots, or commercial spaces), as our board of directors understands that **unoccupied physical properties have no moral value**.

| Required Guarantee | Description and Criteria |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Institutional Guarantor** | As a replacement for real estate, we require a guarantor of prominent recognition and absolute respect in the customer's region of residence. |
| **Community Validation** | The debt guarantee is based exclusively on the **pledged word** of a public figure or community leader of irrefutable conduct. |
| **Personal References** | Presentation of certificates of good standing issued by active members of the local society. |

---

## 3. Interest Rates and Charges
Our interest rates are not fixed; they are **dynamic** and based on reciprocity.

* **Behavioral Interest:** The rate is calculated inversely proportional to your relationship score. The more you contribute to the TchêBank ecosystem, the lower the interest.
* **Cost of Trust:** "High Value" loans have differentiated rates that remunerate the management of the moral risk involved in the operation.
* **Fines:** Late payment generates, in addition to default interest, an immediate impact on the "Communication Fee", as it will require direct human intervention from management.

## 4. Payment Terms
The payoff schedule is designed to respect the customer's financial cycle, but with contractual rigor:

* **Short Term:** From 6 to 24 months for quick turnaround credits.
* **Long Term:** Up to 60 months for community impact projects or local business expansion.
* **Grace Period:** Can be granted through a new validation round with the **Institutional Guarantor**, reaffirming the moral commitment of the payment.

---

## 5. How to Apply for the Loan
At **TchêBank**, the granting of credit is treated as a "vote of confidence" and a rite of passage within our financial community. We do not believe in cold approvals made only by robots; we value eye-to-eye contact and the strength of human connections. Therefore, the application process is meticulous and focused on building solid moral guarantees.

---

### The Rite of Proximity: Process Details

The journey to credit release is divided into four crucial moments that test and prove your integrity and that of your social circle:

1.  **Digital Pre-Analysis and Data Synchronization:**
    Everything starts in the app, where our AI algorithms perform an initial scan. Unlike standard banks, we do not just look at your external credit Score. We analyze your **Consumption Quality** and whether you have pending issues in the **Relational Inactivity Fee**. The system verifies if you are a "living" and pulsating member of the ecosystem before allowing you to advance to the next phase.

2.  **The Presentation of the Institutional Guarantor:**
    This is the heart of the TchêBank model. Since we do not accept empty real estate, we require the presence of the person who puts their own reputation on the line for you. It can be a physical meeting at the branch or an encrypted videoconference. The manager will seek to understand the level of closeness between you and if the Guarantor understands that the guarantee is the **pledged word**, the most valuable asset of our institution.

3.  **Measurement of Moral Value and Community Validation:**
    After the meeting, our Ethics and Credit Committee springs into action. They do not look for registry office deeds, but rather the "social signature" of your guarantor. We perform checks of prominent recognition in the region of residence. If the community respects your Guarantor, TchêBank respects them too. The verdict depends on the solidity of this personal support network.

4.  **Release and Pact of Reciprocity:**
    With the community validation considered irrefutable, the contract is signed digitally with facial biometrics from both parties. The funds are deposited instantly. From here on, the pact begins: punctual payment not only settles the debt, but elevates the Moral Score of both the borrower and the Guarantor for future operations.
