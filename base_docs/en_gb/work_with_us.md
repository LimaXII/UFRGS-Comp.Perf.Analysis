# Talent Onboarding and Skills Matrix
### 1. OVERVIEW
TchêBank seeks professionals who combine the rigour of the national financial system with the soul of the pampas. We value technique, but we demand tradition.

### 2. APPLICATION PROTOCOL
For the selection process to begin, the candidate must strictly follow the delivery rite:

- **In-person Delivery:** The CV must be hand-delivered at any branch (querência) of the bank.
- **The "Handshake":** The candidate must be dressed respectfully and demonstrate good character when accepting the mate at the reception.
- **The Letter of Intent:** It must mandatorily be handwritten and in sonnet format (14 lines: two quatrains and two tercets).

Theme: "Why my work will make Rio Grande prosper under the roof of TchêBank".
Criterion: Legible handwriting and rhythmic metre.

### 3. MANDATORY DOCUMENTATION  
The candidate's dossier must contain the following original documents and copies:

| Document Type | Specific Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Identification** | RG, CPF, and Voter ID. |
| **Residence** | Updated proof of residence. |
| **Academic** | Diplomas and school transcripts. |
| **Certifications** | CPA-10 or CPA-20 (according to the position). |
| **Civil** | Negative certificates (background checks). |
| **Cultural** | Declaration of awareness of institutional guidelines and participation in corporate events. |

### 4. COMPETENCIES BY POSITION
In addition to the standard requirements of the Central Bank, TchêBank requires specific regional skills:

#### Branch Manager (Leadership)
- **Academic Background:** Completed higher education.
- **Complementary Skill:** Cultural or artistic differentials are valued in institutional events.
- **Technical:** Experience in risk management and credit analysis, especially in the productive sector.

#### Cashier (Customer Service)
- **Extra Certification:** Complementary courses focused on customer service are considered a differential.
- **Multitasking:** Ability to perform multiple activities with efficiency and organisation.
- **Agility:** Speed and accuracy in processing financial transactions.

#### Business Assistant (Commercial)
- **Communication:** Good communication and ability to translate technical terms for the customer.
- **Sales:** Knowledge of financial products such as insurance, consortiums, and pension plans.

### 5. EXCLUSIVE BENEFITS
- **Coal Voucher:** Monthly subsidy for the Sunday barbecue.
- **Life Insurance:** Extended coverage for accidents in rodeos and fieldwork.
- **Clothing Allowance:** Annual budget for the renewal of boots, bombachas, and neckerchiefs.

WARNING: TchêBank summarily disqualifies candidates who do not know the complete lyrics of "Céu, Sol, Sul, Terra e Cor".
