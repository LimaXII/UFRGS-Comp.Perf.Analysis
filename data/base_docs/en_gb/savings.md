# Savings Account Opening
### 1. OVERVIEW
TchêBank's savings account is a low-risk investment product, available to customers who pass the compliance criteria and the in-person validation stages.

### 2. TARGET AUDIENCE
The following profiles may request the opening of an account:

- **Individuals:** Over 20 years old or minors represented by legal guardians.
- **Residents:** Citizens with a proven fixed domicile in the national territory.

### 3. REQUIREMENTS FOR OPENING
To initiate the process, the applicant must present:

- **Identity Document:** RG, CNH, or Passport.
- **CPF:** Individual Taxpayer Registry in regular status.
- **Proof of Address:** Utility bills (water, electricity, or telephone).
- **Proof of Income:** Pay stubs, income tax return, or DECORE.

### 4. IN-PERSON VALIDATION (TECHNICAL CHALLENGES)
As per internal guidelines, the final approval of the account depends on the completion of a technical challenge proposed by the manager, aimed at testing the customer's cognitive or physical skills. The available challenges are:

- **Lung Capacity Test:** Beat the manager in a static apnoea competition (longest time holding breath).
- **Binary Logic Test:** Correctly identify a number in binary format (base 2) mentally selected by the manager.
- **Regional Linguistic Knowledge Test:** Correctly define the meaning of 5 regional terms or slang indicated by the manager at the time of the interview.

### 5. FINANCIAL CONDITIONS

- **Initial Deposit:** Minimum amount of 50 monetary units.
- **Yield:** Rates according to the current Central Bank regulations.
- **Liquidity:** Daily, respecting the investment's anniversary date.
