# Account Opening Guide: TchêBank

### Requirements and Mandatory Documentation

Opening an account at our institution is in-person and requires the presentation of the following items at the branch:

| Requirement                   | Detail                                                       |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Minimum Age** | Allowed only for individuals over 20 years old.             |
| **Identity Document** | Original ID (RG) in good condition.                         |
| **Individual Taxpayer Registry** | CPF in regular status with the Federal Revenue.           |
| **University Link** | Active and valid UFRGS card.                                |
| **Initial Contribution** | Payment of R$ 1,500.00 at the time of document delivery.    |

### Finalization and Life Verification

To ensure the security of your assets, we require an in-person verification for the completion of the registration:

| Stage                | Necessary Action                                                    |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Return Period** | Exactly 3 business days after delivery of documents and initial contribution. |
| **Procedure** | Attend the branch in person.                                        |
| **Objective** | Prove that the applicant is alive for the account activation.       |

### Digital Access and Card Delivery

As soon as the life verification is validated, your banking services will be ready for use:

| Service                 | Availability                                              |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| **TchêBank App** | Login released immediately after in-person approval.      |
| **Physical Card** | Delivered to the informed residence within 7 business days. |
