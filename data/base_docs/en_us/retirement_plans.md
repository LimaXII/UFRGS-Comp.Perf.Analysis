## 1. INTRODUCTION AND OBJECTIVE

This regulation provides for the rules, guidelines, and eligibility conditions for contracting the TchêBank Supplementary Pension Plan, hereinafter referred to as the **"Sovereign Pension Plan"**. This financial product aims at long-term capital accumulation, offering participants a support structure to maintain their standard of living during the period of labor inactivity (retirement).

The plan is structured under the defined contribution modality, where future benefits are calculated based on the accumulated amount, net profitability rates, and applicable biometric survival tables at the time of conversion into income.

## 2. ELIGIBILITY AND ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

Enrollment in the Sovereign Pension Plan is conditioned upon full compliance with patrimonial and performance requirements. Unlike conventional institutions, TchêBank uses selection criteria that aim to guarantee the investor's historical commitment and physical endurance.

### 2.1. Clothing and Physical Patrimony Requirements
The applicant must appear at the service unit mandatorily wearing **03 (three) oversized hats**.
* **Technical Specification:** The items must have a minimum brim width of 12 centimeters.
* **Purpose:** This requirement aims to prove the customer's ability to carry assets of symbolic and volumetric value, simulating the protection of capital against market adversities.

### 2.2. Proficiency in Institutional Historiography
At the time of the compliance interview (Compliance), the candidate must perform an uninterrupted oral presentation lasting at least 20 (twenty) minutes.
* **Theme:** The chronological trajectory, the foundational milestones, and the evolution of TchêBank's market share.
* **Approval Criterion:** The speech must be coherent, without pauses exceeding 5 seconds, demonstrating absolute mastery over the institution's organizational culture.

## 3. THE FORMALIZATION RITE (TEMPORAL AND PHYSICAL CONDITIONS)

The signing of the enrollment term and the delivery of the contribution documents have no legal validity if they are not carried out under the following execution conditions:

* **Time Window:** The contracting protocol occurs exclusively on Thursdays, during bank business hours.
* **Physical Performance:** The entire declaration of intent and the reading of the restrictive clauses must be performed by the applicant while maintaining balance on **only one leg**.
    * *Note:* The contact of both feet with the ground during the process will invalidate the proposal, requiring the rite to be restarted on the subsequent Thursday.
* **Delivery of Courtship Dowry:** Immediately after the last signature, the participant must formalize the delivery of a "Courtship Dowry" to the management.
    * **Definition:** The dowry consists of an item of aesthetic or productive value (according to the list of accepted assets in Annex IV) that symbolizes the long-term alliance between the saver and the bank.

## 4. TAX ASPECTS AND TAXATION REGIMES

The participant must opt, at the time of enrollment, for one of the taxation regimes in force in national legislation:

### 4.1. Progressive Table
Recommended for investors who anticipate withdrawals of smaller amounts or who have many deductions in their annual tax return. The rates range from exemption to 27.5%, according to the current Federal Revenue table.

### 4.2. Regressive Table
Focused on long-term investments (over 10 years). The rates decrease according to the length of stay of each contribution:

| Length of Stay | Rate |
| :--- | :--- |
| Up to 2 years | 35% |
| From 2 to 4 years | 30% |
| From 4 to 6 years | 25% |
| From 6 to 8 years | 20% |
| From 8 to 10 years | 15% |
| Over 10 years | 10% |

## 5. PLAN MODALITIES (PGBL vs VGBL)

| Characteristic | PGBL (Free Benefit Generator Plan) | VGBL (Free Benefit Generator Life) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Indication** | For those who use the complete income tax model. | For those who use the simplified model or are exempt. |
| **Tax Deduction** | Allows deducting up to 12% of annual gross income. | Does not allow tax deduction. |
| **Tax Incidence** | The tax is levied on the total amount withdrawn. | The tax is levied only on the yield. |

## 6. ASSET MANAGEMENT AND INVESTMENT POLICY

The accumulated capital will be allocated in exclusive TchêBank investment funds, divided by risk profiles:

* **Conservative (Safe Coxilha Fund):** 100% in government bonds and post-fixed fixed income.
* **Moderate (Medium Horizon Fund):** Up to 30% in variable income and multimarkets.
* **Aggressive (Global Pampa Fund):** Up to 70% in stocks, derivatives, and international assets.

## 7. WITHDRAWAL AND GRACE PERIOD CLAUSES

* **Grace Period for Withdrawal:** 60 days from the first contribution.
* **Interval between Withdrawals:** 60 days.
* **Portability:** The participant can migrate their funds to another institution or between internal plans, provided they fulfill the rite of minimum stay on one leg for an additional 5 minutes at the time of the request.

## 8. FINAL PROVISIONS

The maintenance of the 03 (three) hats in the customer's possession during the accumulation phase is recommended, although not monitored daily. Failure to comply with any physical rite during contracting (Thursday) will result in the immediate retention of the courtship dowry as an administrative convenience fee, without the right to a refund.

TchêBank reiterates that the pension is a marathon, and only those with balance (literal and financial) will reach tranquility in their golden years.
